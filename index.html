The DMI Advantage

DMI PRO

The DMI PRO certification is considered as the Global Standard for Digital Marketing education. The course structure is constantly updated keeping in mind the latest industry trends and practices. The certification equips the students with the know-how along with the insights and experience to work and speak with authority in a fast paced and dynamic industry.

Internationally Curated Program

The DMI PRO curriculum is developed in collaboration with the Global Industry Advisory Council (GIAC) which consists of the top 1% Globally Recognised marketers from companies including Google, Meta, Microsoft, LinkedIN, Twitter, Coca Cola and more. The GIAC involvement makes sure that the program is curated and developed keeping in mind the current industry trends and standard.

International Internship Opportunities

Students who complete the DMI PRO certification will receive the opportunity in applying for an Internship in Australia through a competitive application process. The internship period will range for a period of 8 to 11 months with a stipend of 1,75,000 INR/month.

Globally Recognised Certification

The DMI Program is considered to be the only Globally Recognised and industry accredited and validated digital marketing curriculum that is delivered in over 110 countries across the world. It also carries a globally transferable qualification which is recognised from industry to industry as well as country to country which ensures greater career mobility.

Webinars from International Thought Leaders

The DMI PRO program provides the students with an extensive Knowledge Portal with resources of unparalleled value. Students get access to live, interactive webinars every month from industry leaders around the world on topics which ensures the delivery of industry insights in real time. The access to the DMI community ensures a stronger networking approach and opens up opportunities.

Skillsets Development

The program is designed in a method that cuts out outdated practices and blends in the approach of both theoretical and practical knowledge that helps students develop the required skill sets which are practiced and valued globally across industries. The overall professional development of students allows them to retain the certification and utilize it to open up international opportunities.
